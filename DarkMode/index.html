Bull Terrier

Tail wagging, egghead ipsum. Fetch a stick, chase the ball, zoomies through the yard. A gladiator spirit with a clown's heart. Snuggle on the couch, or bark at the wind. With a strong jaw and a silly grin, this is the best of days.

Tug of war, chew the toy, and burrow deep under blankets. The loyalty is fierce, the mischief is plenty. Ears perked high, a mischievous glint in their eyes. This pup is a white cavalier, full of life and happy sighs.

A powerful build and an agile leap, a nose for adventure while the humans sleep. Needs a walk and a mental game, always ready to repeat the same. Devotion is the game, affection is the win. Now, where is that frisbee? Let the fun begin!

Bull and terrier, the original crossbreed of strength and spunk. Hinks' vision of the perfect gentleman's companion. With a powerful physique and a distinctive egg-shaped head, the White Cavalier roamed the English streets, a dapper dog for the discerning owner.
